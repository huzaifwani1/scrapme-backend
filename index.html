Our Story

About Scrapme

Scrapme was born from a simple idea: those old phones gathering dust in your drawer still have value. We created a platform that makes it incredibly easy to sell your used smartphones — no matter the brand, condition, or age.

Our team of tech enthusiasts and sustainability advocates built Scrapme to bridge the gap between unused devices and fair pricing. We combine AI-powered evaluation with a human touch to ensure every seller gets the best deal.
